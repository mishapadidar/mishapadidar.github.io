About

I am a research fellow in the Center for Computational Mathematics at the Flatiron Institute. Before that, I was a machine learning engineer at Uber. I obtained my Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics from Cornell University, advised by David Bindel. I received a Bachelors of Science from the University of California Santa Barbara, while working with Paul Atzberger.
